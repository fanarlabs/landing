Fanar — builds intelligent applications that seamlessly integrate AI into daily routines. Like the lighthouse guiding ships through darkness, Fanar illuminates the path forward with technology that understands and adapts to human needs.

## Products

### Daylst — AI-Powered Routine Coach
**Status:** Live | **URL:** [daylst.com](https://daylst.com)

Your intelligent daily routine companion that learns, adapts, and evolves with you.

- **AI-Generated Routines** — Tell it your goal; it builds a personalized plan in seconds
- **Behavioral Learning** — Every interaction teaches it more about how you live
- **Energy-Aware Scheduling** — Schedules tasks around your natural energy patterns
- **Version-Controlled Routines** — Track how your routines evolve over time

### Tourida — AI Travel Companion

Your smart travel companion that finds the perfect stays, activities, and adventures tailored to you.

## Tech Preferences (for rebuilding the site)

- **Framework:** Next.js (App Router)
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v4 + shadcn/ui + reactbits
- **Animations:** reactbits or Framer Motion
- **i18n:** next-intl (EN/AR with RTL support)
- **Performance target:** Zero unnecessary dependencies, fast first paint, no heavy bundles
